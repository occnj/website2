Common Questions

Do I need to be a member to have a life event at Oasis?
For baptism, no — anyone who has made a personal faith decision is welcome. For weddings and baby dedications, at least one person should have an established connection to Oasis. Contact us if you're unsure.
Is baptism required to be a member?
We encourage baptism as an important step of faith, but it's not a requirement for belonging to the Oasis community. We believe it's a personal decision made in your own time.
What happens at the baptism info meeting?
It's a relaxed, one-hour conversation with a pastor to talk through what baptism means, what to expect on the day, and answer any questions you have. No pressure, just preparation.
Can family and friends come to watch?
Absolutely — and we encourage it. Baptisms, dedications, and milestone moments are always better shared. Invite your people and celebrate together.
